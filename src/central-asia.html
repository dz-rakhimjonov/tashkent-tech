First Central Asia Conference on Sports Science invites extended abstract submissions to the inaugural meeting, taking place at The New Uzbekistan University

First Central Asia Conference on Sports Science invites extended abstract submissions to the inaugural meeting, taking place at The New Uzbekistan University, Tashkent from April 28 to 30, 2026. The conference aims to showcase high-quality research in sports science and foster collaboration between academics and practitioners. Conference Sponsor: Sports Economics Review Conference Partners: University of Reading; UK Molde University College, Norway We welcome researchers to submit extended abstracts (3 pages) for presentation on a variety of topics within sports science, including but not limited to: 
  • • Sports Economics
  • • Sports Management
  • • Operations Research in Sports
  • • Sports Analytics
  • • Computer Science in Sports
  • • Computer Science in Sports
  • • Sports Psychology
Key Dates: • Extended Abstract Submission Deadline: January 25, 2026 (submit via link https://forms.gle/N7Ykb4co1yrSM78cA ) • Registration will open in February, 2026 • Conference Dates: April 28 - 30, 2026
