Overview

“At Tashkent University of Technology, global partnership is not just a value — it is the foundation of our entire strategy. From day one, we have brought the world to Uzbekistan by focusing on what matters most: people. Our team is made up of faculty members, researchers, and professionals who have received their education and training from leading universities in more than 20 countries, including the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, Japan, South Korea, France, Malaysia, and many others. They bring not only academic excellence but also a global perspective, cross-cultural understanding, and a deep commitment to transforming education and science. Thanks to them, New Uzbekistan University has already become a space where global ideas, intellectual traditions, and best practices come together. On this strong foundation, we are building long-term strategic partnerships with some of the world’s most renowned institutions, including MIT (USA), the University of Cambridge (UK), and the Technical University of Munich (Germany). These alliances allow us not just to adopt best practices, but to co-create new models of education and research — collaboratively, equitably, and with a shared vision for the future of Uzbekistan and the wider region. Our goal is to become a globally recognized university, one that unites talent, knowledge, and technology to tackle the world’s most pressing challenges.
