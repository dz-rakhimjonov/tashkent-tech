Online equivalency calculator

Applicants with an international certificate (SAT, International Baccalaureate (IB), International AS & A Levels) may be exempt from the entrance exam for undergraduate programs. Their math section scores from international certificates will be considered. The online equivalency calculator is made to compare international certificate math section scores with the entrance exam scores. If applicants prefer, they may still take the entrance exam. In case of a difference between the entrance exam score and the score derived from their international certificate, the higher score will be considered.
