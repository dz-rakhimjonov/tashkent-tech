The library of Tashkent University of Technology

Tashkent University of Technology (TTU) NEWUU Library is dedicated to supporting the success of higher education students, educators, and learners by tailoring course materials to their specific requirements. From its humble beginnings as a small library, NUU Library has experienced significant growth, enabling it to meet the increasing educational demands. This expansion involves the provision of high-quality books and the cultivation of a competitive learning environment. To enhance accessibility to online resources, the library offers the use of iMacs and iPads.
