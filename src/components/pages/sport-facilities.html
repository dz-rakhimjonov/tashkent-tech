Sport Facilities

We encourage students to stay active and engaged through a variety of sports facilities available on campus. Our well-equipped spaces allow students to participate in different sports, whether for recreation or competition. Our sports areas are open 24/7 to all students, promoting a healthy lifestyle, teamwork, and personal growth.
