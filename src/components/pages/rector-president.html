Dear students, parents, partners, and friends of Tashkent University of Technology

It is a great honor for me to lead a university that not only delivers academic programs and research initiatives, but also fulfills a national mission to transform the entire education and research ecosystem of Uzbekistan. As the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan has rightly stated, New Uzbekistan University is a flagship institution of a new generation, built for breakthrough built for the future.

I carry a dual responsibility, I serve as both Deputy Head of the Presidential Administration for Education Reform and the Rector of Tashkent University of Technology. These two roles are united by a single vision to create an integrated and progressive educational ecosystem where every child, from kindergarten to university, is supported, inspired, and empowered.

Tashkent University of Technology is a place for the most talented young minds of our nation. Our highly selective admissions process is designed to identify true potential, a passion for learning, and a readiness to act. Our students represent the intellectual elite of Uzbekistan, and I can confidently say: no other university in the country brings together such a concentration of gifted, driven, and ambitious youth.

Rector

Dr. Adham Khudaykulov
