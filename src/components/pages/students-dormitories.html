Student Dormitories of New Uzbekistan University

The New Uzbekistan University provides two student dormitories, offering a comfortable and secure living environment for students during their studies.
Accommodation & Facilities
Dormitory 1: Located within the University of World Economy and Diplomacy student residence.
  • Total Rooms:256
  • Rooms for Female Students: 107
  • Rooms for Male Students: 149
  • Monthly Fee: 640,000 UZS (includes accommodation, heating, electricity, water, and essential services).
Dormitory 2: Located at the Abdulla Avloniy National Institute of Pedagogical Mastery.
  • Capacity: 100 places
  • Features:  Equipped with modern furniture and all necessary facilities for comfortable living and studying.
Room Types:
  • Single rooms (1 person)
  • Double rooms (2 people)
  • Shared rooms (4 people)

How to Apply for Accommodation?
To apply for a dormitory, students must contact the tutor assigned to their course, who will guide them through the application process. Tutors assist in ensuring a smooth transition into student housing.
