General Entry Requirements For All Undergraduate Courses

Educational Background: Applicants must have completed secondary school, academic lyceum, or professional college and provide diploma with academic transcripts. Minimum English Proficiency: IELTS: 5.5 or; CEFR: B2 or; TOEFL iBT: 46 or equivalents (Note: Only TOEFL iBT tests taken at approved test centers are accepted. The TOEFL iBT Home Edition is not accepted.) Entrance Exam Requirement: Applicants must pass the Math & Logical Thinking entrance exam. Applicants with an international certificate (SAT, International Baccalaureate (IB), International AS & A Levels) may be exempt from the entrance exam. Their math section scores from international certificates will be considered. To compare international certificate math scores with the entrance exam scores, applicants may use the online equivalency calculator. If applicants prefer, they may still take the entrance exam. In case of a difference between the entrance exam score and the score derived from their international certificate, the higher score will be considered.
