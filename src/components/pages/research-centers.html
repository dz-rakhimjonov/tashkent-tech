Research Centers

TTU hosts several world-class research centers dedicated to interdisciplinary research aimed at solving complex global challenges. Our flagship centers, such as the Central Asian Center for Development Studies and the Greater Eurasia Research Center (GEAR), are designed to foster innovation, collaboration, and sustainable development in Central Asia and beyond. Each center is a hub for scholars, experts, and students, driving initiatives that impact policy, society, and the environment.
