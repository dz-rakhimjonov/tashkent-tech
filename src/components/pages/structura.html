Structure

The Student Council at Tashkent University of Technology is the official representative body of students, working to enhance student life and ensure their voices are heard. The council plays a key role in fostering leadership, organizing events, and addressing student concerns.
What the Student Council Does:
Represents Students – Acts as a bridge between students and university administration.
Organizes Events& Initiatives – Plans social, academic, and cultural activities.
Advocates for Student Rights – Addresses student concerns and proposes improvements.
Promotes Leadership & Engagement – Encourages students to participate in university life.
