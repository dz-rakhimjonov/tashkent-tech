Exam Specification

The Tashkent University of Technology Entrance Exam is conducted in collaboration with Cambridge University Press & Assessment. This exam is designed to assess candidates’ mathematical skills and logical thinking abilities to determine their eligibility for admission. What’s Included in the Exam Specification? The Exam Specification document provides detailed information on:

  1. Test structure and format
  2. Topics covered in the exam
  3. Sample questions to help candidates prepare effectively
